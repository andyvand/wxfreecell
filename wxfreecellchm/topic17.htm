Overview of FreeCell

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FreeCell is a logic puzzle in the form of a solitaire card game.  It is unlike most solitaire games, however, in that there is no luck involved after the initial shuffle.  There are no hidden cards; they are all dealt face up at the start of the game.  It is believed (although not proven) that every game is winnable.
